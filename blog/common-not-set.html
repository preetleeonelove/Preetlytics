Understanding 'Not Set'

'Not Set' appears in GA4 when certain data points are missing in your reports, often due to improper configuration or missing tags.

Common Causes:

  • Missing event parameters or tracking codes.
  • Improperly configured data streams.
  • Users with blocked cookies or ad blockers.
  • Incorrect implementation in GTM.

Regular audits and proper tag management help eliminate most 'Not Set' issues, providing accurate reporting.
