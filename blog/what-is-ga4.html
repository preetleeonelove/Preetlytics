Introduction to GA4

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is the latest version of Google Analytics, designed to provide more accurate and granular tracking of website and app activity.

Key Features:

  • Event-based tracking instead of session-based.
  • Enhanced cross-device and cross-platform analytics.
  • Integration with Google Ads and BigQuery.
  • Improved reporting for user engagement and retention.

GA4 helps you make data-driven decisions by offering a complete view of user behavior and actionable insights.
