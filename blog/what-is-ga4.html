Audit Your GA4 Setup

GA4 audits are essential to ensure accurate tracking of events, conversions, and user interactions. Regular audits help identify missing events, misconfigured parameters, and discrepancies in reporting.

Steps for GA4 Audit:

  • Check property settings and data streams.
  • Validate events, conversions, and parameters.
  • Verify tag implementation through GTM or site code.
  • Test eCommerce tracking if applicable.
  • Monitor reports and debug with GA4 DebugView.

Performing audits ensures your data is reliable for making informed business decisions.
